BLACK BEAN-GOAT CHEESE QUESADILLAS
¾ c. shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese ½ c. crumbled goat cheese,1/4 c. chopped fresh cilantro, 1 tbsp. chili powder, and 1 (15 oz.) can drained black beans. Spread ¾ c. bean mixture over each of 4 (6 in.) fat-free tortillas. Top each with another tortilla, lightly press. Cook quesadillas in nonstick skillet over high heat for 2 min. or until each side is browned. Serves 4.



GRACES'S SALMON

2 salmon fillet and 1 thinly sliced lemon Thinly sliced lemon Rub the salmon in olive oil and salt and pepper and place the lemon slices on top. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 min with heat and five min with heat off
